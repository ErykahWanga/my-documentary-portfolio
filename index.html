Biography

Born: Nairobi, Kenya
Profession: Documentary Photographer

Erykah Wanga is a documentary photographer from Nairobi, Kenya, known for capturing powerful, untold stories of everyday life. Her work focuses on human resilience, environmental challenges, and marginalized communities. Inspired by Nairobi’s vibrant culture and Kenya's diverse landscapes, she uses photography to raise awareness and inspire change. Erykah’s acclaimed projects have earned her recognition both locally and internationally, making her a leading voice in contemporary documentary photography.

Photo

Challenges

Throughout her career, Erykah has faced many challenges, from limited access to remote communities to the difficulties of working in unstable environments. However, her commitment to capturing untold stories keeps her pushing forward despite these obstacles.

Achievements

  • Featured in National Geographic
  • Winner of the 2020 Documentary Photography Award
  • Collaborated with NGOs to highlight climate change impacts in Africa
